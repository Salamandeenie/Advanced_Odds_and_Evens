1. First player clicks 'Join' and sends the GameID to the second player.
  2. Second player enters the GameID, and the game is started.
  3. This is a more advanced version of Odds and Evens.
  4. Players take turns manipulating a secret number, with the number being revealed on turn #20.
  5. The first player is Odds, and the second player is Evens.
  6. On their turn, players may perform one of the following operations:
    • Addition (+): They can add any positive or negative number to the secret number.
    • Subtraction (-): They can subtract any positive or negative number from the secret number.
    • Modulo (%): They can take the modulo of the secret number with any positive number.
    • Absolute Value (||): They can take the absolute value of the secret number, disregarding its sign.
    • Rerandomization (?): They can introduce a small random adjustment to the secret number.
    • Peek (~): They may forfeit a turn, and in return they may see the secret number.
  7. Players continue to take turns manipulating the secret number until turn #20, when the number is revealed.
  8. After the secret number is revealed, the player to whom the secret number refers wins!
  9. Win conditions:
    • For the Odd player to win:
      • The secret number can be a positive odd.
      • The secret number can be a negative even.
    • For the Even player to win:
      • The secret number can be a positive even.
      • The secret number can be a negative odd.
